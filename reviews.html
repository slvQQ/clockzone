MSI Trident A * * * * (4STARS)
VERDICT – Small and almost perfectly formed, this tiny gaming PC packs a 1080p and 1440p punch inside a smart, quiet enclosure.
PROS – Solid 1080p and 1440p gaming / Compact design / Well-balanced components / Very quiet
CONS - RTX 2060 Super is faster / A little pricey compared to conventional PCs / Some suspect build quality

Alienware Aurora R8 * * * * (4.5 STARS)
VERDICT – If you’re starting your build from scratch and fancy the strongest gaming desktop possible, the Alienware Aurora R8 is a fantastic candidate
PROS – Top-of-the-line performance / Fantastic value / Easy to upgrade / Ray tracing and DLSS support
CONS - Overpowered CPU drives price up needlessly

Asus ROG Strix GL 12CX * * * (3.5 STARS)
VERDICT – The Asus GL 12CX is worth a look for those seeking a compact but capable gaming PC, but it isn't without its foibles.
PROS – Masses of performance / Fast components across the board / Compact case design / Easy to upgrade / Has optical drive
CONS - Noisy cooling fan / Expensive

MSI Trident X * * * * (4.5 STARS)
VERDICT – TIncredible speeds and smart design, but it's expensive – and more conventional PCs are more versatile.
PROS – Superb CPU power / Fast Turing GPU / Small, good-looking enclosure / 32GB of RAM
CONS - Expensive / Underwhelming motherboard / No easy upgrade paths

Acer Predator Orion 9000 * * * (3.5 STARS)
VERDICT – A high price and love it or loath it design makes the Orion 9000 a hard sell right now.
PROS – Masses of performance / Plenty of upgrade potential / Ample cooling
CONS - Too bulky / A bit noisy / Overkill for most gamers

Zotac MEK1 * * * * (4.5 STARS)
PROS – Small, sturdy design / Rapid gaming performance / Quiet operation / Reasonable price
CONS - No real expansion potential / Middling Core i7 CPU
